I Don't Have an Olin Slack Account

If you have a @olin.edu or @student.olin.edu email address, you can use that to create a Slack account, and use that to sign in here. Visit olin.slack.com to create an account.

You can also sign in by email (experimental).

Why Do I Need to Sign In?

This lets us know that you're in the Olin Community. We'll show you Olin community events, and we'll trust you to add and edit events on the calendar.

You don't need to sign in (a) if you're on campus, or (b) to view only public events.
